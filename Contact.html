Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours? ‚ñº

We deliver fresh produce daily from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday through Saturday, and 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM on Sundays. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 10:00 AM.

How do I become a farmer partner? ‚ñº

We welcome farmers to join our platform! Simply visit our "Become a Seller" page or contact us at support@farmfresh.com. Our team will guide you through the registration process and help you get started with selling your produce directly to customers.

What is your return policy? ‚ñº

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the quality of your produce, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund, no questions asked.

Do you offer organic certification? ‚ñº

Yes! All our organic products are certified by recognized certification bodies. You can view the certification details on each product page. We work only with verified farmers who follow organic farming practices.

What payment methods do you accept? ‚ñº

We accept all major payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash on delivery. All online transactions are secure and encrypted for your safety.
